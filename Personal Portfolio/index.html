My Story

a little bit about Naveen

Hello! I'm Navile Nageshwara Naveen, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering with over 12 years of dedicated experience in teaching and guiding students. My passion lies in exploring the realms of IoT, Android App Development, OOPs Concepts, and Problem Solving Techniques. Throughout my career, I've been committed to fostering a dynamic learning environment where students can thrive and excel. I take pride in imparting not just knowledge but also practical skills that prepare my students for real-world challenges. My journey in academia has been marked by a relentless pursuit of innovation and a deep-seated belief in the transformative power of education. I am driven by a vision to empower the next generation of computer scientists and engineers to make meaningful contributions to society through technology.

My goal is to leverage my extensive experience to inspire and empower students in their academic and professional pursuits. I am committed to fostering a dynamic learning environment where students can develop not only technical proficiency but also critical thinking skills, creativity, and a passion for lifelong learning. Ultimately, my goal is to make a positive impact on the world by equipping individuals with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in the rapidly evolving field of computer science.
